Omens

Powerful crafting items - Found in Ritual encounters - Purchased with Tribute

How Omens Work

Use an Omen by right-clicking it in your inventory. When it glows, it's ready to apply to the next use of the appropriate Orb. Omens are consumed on use.

Orb of Alchemy Omens

Affect your next Orb of Alchemy usage

Omen of Sinistral Alchemy

Crafting Omen

Your next Orb of Alchemy will result in the maximum number of Prefix modifiers. The item will gain as many Prefixes as possible (up to 3) when upgraded to Rare.

Stack: 10 Prefix Alchemy
10

Omen of Dextral Alchemy

Crafting Omen

Your next Orb of Alchemy will result in the maximum number of Suffix modifiers. The item will gain as many Suffixes as possible (up to 3) when upgraded to Rare.

Stack: 10 Suffix Alchemy
10

Regal Orb Omens

Affect your next Regal Orb usage

Omen of Sinistral Coronation

Crafting Omen

Your next Regal Orb will add only Prefix modifiers. Guarantees that the new modifier added when upgrading a Magic item to Rare will be a Prefix.

10

Omen of Dextral Coronation

Crafting Omen

Your next Regal Orb will add only Suffix modifiers. Guarantees that the new modifier added when upgrading a Magic item to Rare will be a Suffix.

Stack: 10 Suffix Regal
10

Vaal Orb Omens

Affect your next Vaal Orb usage

Omen of Corruption

Crafting Omen

Your next Vaal Orb will be more unpredictable. Increases the chance of powerful outcomes when corrupting an item, but also increases the risk of bricking it.

10

Defensive & Utility Omens

Activated upon reaching low life or special conditions

Omen of Resurgence

Defensive Omen

While this item is active in your inventory, you will fully recover your Life, Mana, and Energy Shield when you reach low life. Consumed on trigger.

Stack: 10 Recovery Low Life
10

Omen of Refreshment

Utility Omen

While this item is active in your inventory, you will fully recover your Flask and Charm charges when you reach low life. Consumed on trigger.

Stack: 10 Flasks Charges
10

Omen of Amelioration

Defensive Omen

While this item is active in your inventory, prevents 75% of experience loss when you die. Consumed on death. Essential for high-level mapping.

Stack: 10 Experience Death Protection
10

Where to Find Omens

Omens are purchased with Tribute from Ritual encounters in endgame maps. Ritual altars appear in maps and allow you to spend Tribute to buy omens and other rewards.
