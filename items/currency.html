Used to buy items from vendors and respec the Passive Skill Tree. Gold is the primary trading currency with NPCs and is used for gambling and crafting services.

Quality Currency

Each point of quality increases the item's main attribute by 1%

Armourer's Scrap

Quality Currency

Increases the quality of an Armour by 1% per scrap (up to 20% maximum). Obtained by salvaging items with quality or disenchanting equipment.

Stack: 40 Armour Quality
40

Blacksmith's Whetstone

Quality Currency

Increases the quality of a Martial Weapon (Swords, Axes, Maces, etc.) by 1% per whetstone (up to 20% maximum). Obtained by salvaging items with quality.

Stack: 20 Weapon Quality
20

Arcanist's Etcher

Quality Currency

Increases the quality of a Wand, Staff, or Sceptre by 1% per etcher (up to 20% maximum). Essential for caster builds.

Stack: 20 Caster Quality
20

Gemcutter's Prism

Quality Currency

Crafting Orbs

Core currency for modifying items and creating powerful equipment

Orb of Transmutation

Crafting Orb

Upgrades a Normal (white) item to a Magic (blue) item with 1 random modifier. Drops from monsters or obtained by disenchanting magic equipment.

Stack: 40 Normal → Magic
40

Orb of Augmentation

Crafting Orb

Adds a new random modifier to a Magic (blue) item. Magic items can have up to 2 modifiers total.

Stack: 30 Add Mod
30

Orb of Alchemy

Crafting Orb

Upgrades a Normal (white) item to a Rare (yellow) item with 4 random modifiers. Essential for endgame crafting.

Stack: 20 Normal → Rare
20

Chaos Orb

Crafting Orb

Removes a random modifier from a Rare item and adds a new random modifier. The primary currency for trading between players.

Stack: 20 Reroll
20

Exalted Orb

Crafting Orb

Adds a new random modifier to a Rare item. Rare items can have up to 6 modifiers total. One of the most valuable crafting orbs.

Stack: 20 Add Mod
20

Divine Orb

Crafting Orb

Randomizes the numeric values of all modifiers on an item within their ranges. Used to perfect the rolls on gear.

Stack: 10 Reroll Values
10

Regal Orb

Crafting Orb

Upgrades a Magic (blue) item to a Rare (yellow) item, keeping existing modifiers and adding one new random modifier.

Stack: 20 Magic → Rare
20

Orb of Annulment

Crafting Orb

Removes a random modifier from an item. Used in advanced crafting to selectively remove unwanted mods.

Stack: 20 Remove Mod
20

Orb of Chance

Crafting Orb

Randomly upgrades a Normal (white) item to a Unique (orange) item of the same base type, or destroys it. A high-risk, high-reward crafting tool.

Stack: 10 Gamble Unique
10

Vaal Orb

Corruption

Corrupts an item with unpredictable results. Can add powerful modifiers, reroll values, add sockets, or destroy the item. Corrupted items cannot be modified further. Drops from Vaal areas and Trial of Chaos.

Stack: 20 Corrupt Risky
20

Fracturing Orb

Special

Fractures a random modifier on a Rare item that has at least 4 modifiers, locking it permanently. Fractured modifiers cannot be changed by any crafting method.

Stack: 10 Endgame Lock Mod
10

Mirror of Kalandra

Special

Creates a mirrored copy of any non-Unique item. Mirrored copies are exact duplicates but cannot be modified. The rarest and most valuable currency in the game.

Stack: 10 Ultra Rare Mirror
10

Socket Currency

Adds a Rune Socket to equipment (Martial Weapons, Wands, Staffs, Armour). Maximum 2 sockets on two-handed weapons and body armour, 1 on everything else. Obtained by salvaging socketed equipment.

Stack: 20 Rune Socket
20

Adds a Support Gem Socket to a Skill Gem that currently has 2 Support Sockets (increasing it to 3 total sockets). Drops from monsters and league mechanics.

20

Greater Jeweller's Orb

Gem Currency

Adds a Support Gem Socket to a Skill Gem that currently has 3 Support Sockets (increasing it to 4 total sockets).

Stack: 20 3 → 4 Sockets
20

Perfect Jeweller's Orb

Gem Currency

Adds a Support Gem Socket to a Skill Gem that currently has 4 Support Sockets (increasing it to 5 total sockets - maximum). The rarest gem socket currency.

Stack: 20 4 → 5 Sockets
