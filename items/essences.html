Essences

Guaranteed modifiers for crafting - Reworked in Patch 0.3.0 - 84 total essences

Essence Rework (Patch 0.3.0)

• Essences now have 4 tiers: Lesser, Normal, Greater, and Perfect
• Lesser, Normal, and Greater Essences upgrade Magic items to Rare with a guaranteed modifier
• Perfect Essences remove a random modifier and add a new guaranteed modifier to Rare items
• Essences are obtained by defeating enemies imprisoned in essences during Campaigns
