Weapon System Notes

Two Weapon Loadouts

In PoE 2, players can use two separate weapon loadouts, allowing you to equip and switch between different types of weapons during combat. [citation:6]

Not Class-Locked

Weapons are not tied to any specific class. The only aspect that determines whether a character can equip a certain weapon type is a predetermined prerequisite stat. [citation:6]
