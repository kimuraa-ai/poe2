Distilled Emotions

Delirium-exclusive currency - Anoint Amulets or Instill Waystones with Delirium

What Are Distilled Emotions?

Two Main Uses:

1. Anoint Amulets: Combine three Distilled Emotions in the Instilling window to add a Notable Passive Skill from the Passive Skill Tree to your amulet. The amulet grants that passive as long as it's equipped.

2. Instill Waystones: Apply up to three Distilled Emotions to a Waystone to add Delirium layers. Each emotion increases Delirium percentage and adds specific modifiers to the map.

How to Use Distilled Emotions

Anoint Amulets

Right-click a Distilled Emotion to open the Instilling window. Combine three emotions to add a Notable Passive Skill to your amulet. The amulet grants that passive as long as it's equipped. Different combinations create different anointments.

Instill Waystones

Apply up to three Distilled Emotions to a Waystone to add Delirium layers. Each emotion adds a specific modifier to the map and increases the Delirium percentage. Higher Delirium = more rewards but also more danger.

Where to Find Distilled Emotions

Distilled Emotions drop from Delirium encounters in endgame maps. The rarity of the emotion corresponds to the difficulty of the encounter. Higher Delirium percentages yield rarer emotions. Distilled Isolation is the rarest and drops from the highest delirium levels (80%+).
