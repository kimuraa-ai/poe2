WARRIOR

STRENGTH
🔨 Mace

The Unstoppable Force

The Warrior is the embodiment of raw physical power in Path of Exile 2. Wielding massive maces with devastating force, Warriors dominate the battlefield through sheer strength and resilience.

Specializing in close-quarters combat, Warriors excel at breaking enemy lines, controlling crowds, and delivering crushing blows that can shatter armor and bones alike. Their playstyle revolves around powerful strikes, tactical positioning, and overwhelming physical presence.

With the highest base life and armor in the game, Warriors can withstand tremendous punishment while dishing out even more damage. They are the frontline fighters who charge headfirst into battle, creating opportunities for their allies while crushing their enemies.

Warrior Abilities

Ground Slam

Area Attack

Slam your mace into the ground, creating a powerful shockwave that damages and stuns all enemies in a cone in front of you. The shockwave travels along the ground, hitting multiple targets.

AoE Stun Physical

Heavy Strike

Single Target

A powerful overhead strike that deals massive damage to a single enemy. Has increased chance to stun and knocks enemies back, creating space or setting up for follow-up attacks.

Knockback Stun Single Target

Enduring Cry

Warcry

Let out a mighty warcry that taunts nearby enemies, forcing them to attack you. Generates endurance charges that reduce incoming damage and increase your resilience.

Taunt Defensive Buff

Leap Slam

Movement

Leap through the air and slam down at a target location, damaging and stunning enemies in a small area upon landing. Excellent for initiating combat or escaping dangerous situations.

Movement AoE Stun

Warrior Subclasses

HERALD OF WAR

The Inspiring Leader

The Herald of War is a battlefield commander who inspires allies and demoralizes enemies. This subclass focuses on aura effects, team support, and tactical advantages rather than pure personal strength.

Key Features

  • War Banners that provide massive buffs to allies
  • Powerful warcries that boost party damage and defense
  • Bonuses when fighting near allies or against outnumbered foes
  • Enhanced aura effectiveness and larger area of effect
  • Superior battlefield control with enhanced taunt abilities

TITAN

The Unbreakable Colossus

The Titan is the ultimate embodiment of physical might and durability. This subclass focuses on maximum defense, unstoppable momentum, and overwhelming single-target damage through massive critical strikes.

Key Features

  • Massive critical hits that can one-shot most enemies
  • Cannot be stunned, frozen, or knocked back
  • Enormous bonus to armor and maximum life
  • Attacks cause earthquakes that damage nearby enemies
  • Gains stacking damage buffs the longer you stay in combat

KITAVA'S SMITH

The Demonic Blacksmith

A warrior infused with the power of Kitava, the god of war. This subclass combines raw physical power with demonic energy, using cursed weapons and blood magic to dominate the battlefield.

Key Features

  • Weapons imbued with demonic energy and curses
  • Blood magic that sacrifices health for immense power
  • Fire and chaos damage added to physical attacks
  • Life leech and life regeneration from damage dealt
  • Summon demonic weapons and armor during combat
