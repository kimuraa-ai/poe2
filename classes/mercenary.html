MERCENARY

STRENGTH DEXTERITY
⚔️ Sword & Shield

The Professional Warrior

The Mercenary is a versatile combat specialist who excels at tactical warfare and adaptive combat in Path of Exile 2. Wielding sword and shield with military precision, he employs various combat disciplines to overcome any challenge.

Specializing in tactical positioning, counter-magic, and gem-enhanced combat, Mercenaries excel at outsmarting opponents rather than overpowering them. Their playstyle revolves around preparation, adaptation, and exploiting specific enemy weaknesses.

With training in multiple combat disciplines and access to specialized techniques, the Mercenary can counter spellcasters, employ battlefield tactics, or enhance his abilities with gem technology, making him the ultimate adaptable warrior.

Mercenary Ascendancy Classes

TACTICIAN

Master of Battlefield Strategy

The Tactician specializes in battlefield control, positioning, and strategic advantages. Masters the art of manipulating combat flow, setting up advantageous positions, and outmaneuvering opponents through superior tactics.

Key Features

  • Increased effect of banners and auras
  • Bonus damage against outnumbered enemies
  • Tactical positioning bonuses for defense
  • Command skills affect larger areas
  • Bonus to impale and fortify effects
  • Strategic retreat for defensive bonuses

WITCHHUNTER

Hunter of Magic Users

The Witchhunter specializes in countering magic users, disrupting spells, and hunting down spellcasters. Masters anti-magic techniques, silence effects, and abilities that punish magical opponents.

Key Features

  • Bonus damage against spellcasting enemies
  • Spell suppression and disruption
  • Mana burn effects on attacks
  • Silence and curse immunity
  • Reflect spell damage to casters
  • Detect magical enemies from distance

GEMLING LEGIONNAIRE

Gem-Enhanced Soldier

The Gemling Legionnaire uses gem technology to enhance combat abilities, incorporating skill gems directly into their physiology. Masters gem socketing, gem synergy, and artificial enhancement through gem implantation.

Key Features

  • Additional gem sockets on gear
  • Gem effects have increased potency
  • Socketed gems provide passive bonuses
  • Gem combination synergies
  • Gem-powered transformation abilities
  • Resistance to chaos damage from gems
