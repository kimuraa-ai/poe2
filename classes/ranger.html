RANGER

DEXTERITY
🏹 Bow

Master of the Wilds

The Ranger is a master of ranged combat and wilderness survival in Path of Exile 2. Wielding bows with unparalleled precision, she strikes enemies from a distance while maintaining superior mobility and positioning.

Specializing in evasion, critical strikes, and projectile damage, Rangers excel at kiting enemies, clearing packs with piercing shots, and dealing massive damage to single targets. Their playstyle revolves around movement, positioning, and precise aiming.

With the highest base evasion and critical strike chance in the game, the Ranger can avoid damage while delivering devastating blows, making her one of the most agile and deadly classes in Wraeclast.

Ranger Abilities

Ice Shot

Cold Arrow

Fire an arrow that deals cold damage and creates a chilling area on impact. Enemies hit are chilled, and those killed have a chance to shatter.

Cold Projectile AoE

Lightning Arrow

Lightning Arrow

Fire an arrow that converts physical damage to lightning damage. On hit, creates arcs of lightning that chain to nearby enemies.

Lightning Projectile Chaining

Barrage

Rapid Fire

Rapidly fire multiple arrows in a narrow cone. Each arrow can hit the same target, making it devastating against single enemies and bosses.

Physical Projectile Single Target

Blink Arrow

Movement & Summon

Fire an arrow that creates a clone of yourself at the target location, then teleport to that location. The clone attacks nearby enemies.

Movement Summon Utility

Ranger Ascendancy Classes

DEADEYE

Sniper - Снайпер

The Deadeye specializes in projectile attacks, accuracy, and critical strikes. Masters the art of ricocheting projectiles, chain attacks, and piercing shots that hit multiple targets with pinpoint accuracy.

Key Features

  • Projectiles chain to additional enemies
  • Massive critical strike chance and multiplier
  • Projectiles pierce through multiple targets
  • Increased accuracy and projectile speed
  • Additional projectile on all attacks
  • Far Shot for increased damage at distance

PATHFINDER

Pathfinder - Следопыт

The Pathfinder specializes in flask usage, elemental damage, and poison. Masters the art of sustaining powerful flasks and dealing elemental and chaos damage over time while traversing dangerous terrain.

Key Features

  • Flasks have increased effect and duration
  • Elemental damage with attack skills
  • Poison and chaos damage over time
  • Nature's adrenaline for speed bonuses
  • Master surgeon for life recovery
  • Elemental penetration with flasks active
