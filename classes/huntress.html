HUNTRESS

DEXTERITY
🏹 Spear

Master of the Hunt

The Huntress is a tribal warrior who masters both spear combat and ritual magic in Path of Exile 2. She combines primal strength with ancient rituals to become a formidable force on the battlefield.

Specializing in spear attacks, totems, and elemental damage, Huntresses excel at both ranged and melee combat while supporting their attacks with powerful tribal rituals. Their playstyle revolves around positioning, totem placement, and elemental combinations.

With the ability to summon totems, imbue weapons with elemental power, and perform devastating spear throws, the Huntress adapts to any combat situation with primal ferocity.

Huntress Abilities

Spear Throw

Ranged Attack

Hurl your spear at enemies, dealing physical damage and impaling them. The spear can be recalled, dealing additional damage on return.

Physical Projectile Impale

Ancestral Totem

Totem Summon

Summon an ancestral totem that empowers your attacks and increases your attack speed. Multiple totems can be active simultaneously.

Totem Buff Summon

Elemental Imbuement

Elemental Buff

Imbue your weapon with elemental power, adding fire, cold, or lightning damage to your attacks for a duration.

Elemental Buff Duration

Primal Leap

Movement

Leap to a target location, creating a shockwave that damages and staggers enemies upon landing. Can be used while holding a spear.

Movement AoE Stun

Huntress Ascendancy Classes

AMAZON

Spear Mastery Specialist

The Amazon specializes in spear and javelin combat, with enhanced throwing abilities and battlefield mobility. Masters the art of impaling enemies and controlling space with precise spear attacks.

Key Features

  • Enhanced spear throwing range and damage
  • Impale enemies for additional damage
  • Increased movement and attack speed
  • Spear attacks have chance to pierce
  • Javelin skills with elemental effects
  • Dodge and evasion bonuses

RITUALIST

Master of Tribal Rituals

The Ritualist specializes in totem magic and elemental rituals, summoning powerful ancestral spirits to aid in battle. Masters the art of totem placement and elemental imbuing.

Key Features

  • Summon multiple powerful totems
  • Totems provide aura effects and buffs
  • Elemental ritual circles for area control
  • Ancestral spirits that fight alongside you
  • Ritual empowerment for increased damage
  • Elemental resistance penetration
