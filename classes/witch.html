WITCH

INTELLIGENCE
🔮 Wand

Mistress of Dark Arts

The Witch is a master of dark magic, forbidden rituals, and blood magic in Path of Exile 2. Wielding powerful wands and sacrificial daggers, she commands demonic forces and manipulates life essence.

Specializing in blood magic, demon summoning, and necromancy, Witches excel at sacrificing health for power and controlling the battlefield with dark rituals. Their playstyle focuses on risk-reward mechanics and overwhelming enemies with dark energies.

With access to life manipulation, demonic pacts, and undeath, the Witch can become an unstoppable force by embracing forbidden knowledge and making dark bargains.

Witch Abilities

Demonic Pact

Summoning Ritual

Sacrifice a portion of your health to summon a powerful demon that fights for you. The demon's strength scales with the amount of health sacrificed.

Demon Sacrifice Minion

Blood Boil

Blood Magic

Cause enemies' blood to boil, dealing chaos damage over time. The damage increases based on your missing health percentage.

Chaos Blood Damage Over Time

Soul Harvest

Life Manipulation

Drain life force from nearby enemies, healing you for a portion of the damage dealt. Enemies killed by this skill explode, damaging others.

Life Drain AoE Healing

Dark Ritual

Sacrificial Spell

Sacrifice a percentage of your maximum health to create a powerful curse area that reduces enemy resistances and deals chaos damage.

Curse Sacrifice Chaos

Witch Ascendancy Classes

INFERNALIST

Master of Demonic Flames

The Infernalist specializes in fire magic fueled by demonic pacts and sacrifices. Masters the art of summoning demons and wielding hellfire to burn enemies to ash.

Key Features

  • Summon powerful fire demons and imps
  • Fire damage converts to chaos damage
  • Demonic pacts grant fire resistance penetration
  • Ignited enemies spread fire to nearby foes
  • Sacrifice health for massive fire damage bursts

BLOODMAGE

Weaver of Life Essence

The Bloodmage manipulates life force, using blood as both weapon and defense. Specializes in sacrificing health for immense power and controlling the battlefield through life manipulation.

Key Features

  • Spells cost health instead of mana
  • Massive life regeneration and leech
  • Blood rituals empower spells
  • Convert maximum mana to maximum life
  • Blood explosions on enemy death

LICH

Undead Archmage

The Lich embraces undeath, trading mortality for eternal power. Masters necromancy and soul manipulation, becoming a powerful undead spellcaster with unique undead mechanics.

Key Features

  • Convert life to energy shield permanently
  • Summon undead knights and mages
  • Soul manipulation for buffs and debuffs
  • Cannot be stunned or frozen
  • Gain power from nearby corpses
